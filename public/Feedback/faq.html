FAQs

  • Why does the page look weird? Not properly organised
    My apologies, I have tried to make it responsive but I was unable to. I suggest you using a laptop to view the site, if anything is not proper. As I used a Macbook to make the site, I am positive the dimensions are based of that
  • Why are some information not found?
    I have chosen not to include information that goes too in depth into the story / speaks about major plot points. As this is a blog to give you a bit of information to get you started on the anime. I could add spoiler tags and add in more information but that would just put risks of spoiling curious users.
    However , if wanted , do let me know I will do my best in adding them.
  • Why are there so many spotify iFrames?
    Its mainly there for you to listen to a bit of the music and see if it's your taste. Due to the fact the music being very different from each other, if I only linked a few songs you would not get the proper experience. I do know you may experience lag loading the iFrames but do bear with it
  • Is the feedback form the only way of contact?
    No! You can contact me at my socials as mentioned on the About Me page
  • Why are these information so familiar? Can they be found elsewhere?
    Yes they can, however my site has consolidated information from many different sites. I have rephrased and retyped all of the content so that it is easier to understand and get into the anime/musician. You may find these information at other sites but they are all spread out and phrased differently that may make it hard for people to understand
  • What about copyright? I don't see any credit given on your sites.
    I have tried my best to give all credit on the site / acknowledgement page. Also if the owner is not happy with my usage, I will just gladly replace it / take it down.
