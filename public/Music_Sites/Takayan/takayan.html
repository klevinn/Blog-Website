About the Music he produces

  • He started off as a Utaite. A Utaite is someone who posts covers of previously released songs on Youtube or Nico Nico Douga
  • Here is a link that talks more about it.
  • Takayan has been producing his own music since 2015 on top of making covers.
  • Takayan produces everything himself, he produces the lyrics, music, instrumentals et cetera. He does occasionally use instrumentals by other artists, however they are properly creditted and he has been allowed to do so.
  • He was in a group called Stupid Guys with Domura Riu. However I cannot find any songs produced by them anymore.

 
  • Nowadays he makes original songs, though they may be explicit, many of his songs are targetted at real life problems. He still makes covers / remixes of covers but not as often as he focuses more on original music
  • He posts once a week showcasing the lyric videos of the songs he produced and many other things on his Youtube Channel
  • Takayan's lyrics mainly speak about the matters of depression and how many people are facing it. His genre of lyrics normally head down into a dark route tackling real life problems that may be relateable to adolescent teens and et cetera.
  • His lyrics are also depicting more of things that can cause these mental stress such as depression. He has many songs about cheating, suic*de and other things related to depression.
  • He sings for the LGTBQ+ community, however he himself is heterosexual.
  • Its also said that his lyrics are 60% Experience 40% Fantasy

I believe his lyrics aim to raise awareness of the distress these victims face through a form of media that many people can indulge in. Its a type of media also where people don't really need to be hands-on and just listen.

This is one of my favourite tweets of his is where he says that "Instead of taking drugs, take a dose of my music"

Personal Opinions of Takayan

Takayan is an extremely talented rapper.He has extremely good sense in music. I like all of his songs. He is able to properly convey the message of his songs in his lyrics and he is also able to properly use the beat and instrumental to make the music more enjoyable for the people listening. His lyrics also hit home for me and probably a lot of other people, which makes the music more enjoyable and relateable. His carefree personality on his Instagram lives and et cetera makes him very comforting at times. I highly recommend listening to his music especially if you are going through a tough time.


My favourite song of his was Want a boyfriend but it has been removed from spotify.
However my close second is Everyone is Trash

Recommendation: Kamiyama Yoh
His music is more slow paced as compared to Takayan but his lyrics are similar in terms of tackling problems that are faced by teenagers.
