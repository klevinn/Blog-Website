Easter Eggs

These References/Easter Eggs are found throughout the whole Anime

  • Toradora References (Made By Same Studio)

    1. Rita Ainsworth is just Ami Kawashima from Toradora
      Their appearance are similar in terms of Rita just being a blonde version of Ami. (Similar Hairstyle and et cetera)
      They are also similar in personality as they are the types to consider themselves pretty girls
    2. Ryuunosuke and Ryuuji both have names relating to the Japanese word for Dragon, Ryuu 竜
      Also sharing the same trait of them both being social rejects, at the beginning they both only have like 1 'friend'
    3. Misaki Kamiigusa and Minori Kushieda are just the same girl different appearance?!?!
      They both have similar personalities of acting crazy / screaming a lot without feeling a shred of embarrassment. They both hide their true selves as well / like to face things in private instead
    4. Aoyama Nanami's favourite plush toy is just a reference to Taiga the chatacter from Toradora.
      Aoyama's favourite plus toy being a Tiger, and Taiga, known as the Palmtop Tiger. The link could not be more obvious!

  • Initial D / Fast and Furious References

    There is a particular scene in Sakurasou that references / similar to any racing anime or show

  • Lastly other animes have also referenced Sakurasou No Pet Na Kanojo

    Seishun Buta Yarou wa Bunny Girl Senpai no Yume wo Minai, AKA Rascal Does Not Dream of Bunny Girl Senpai, referenced the school in Sakurasou No Pet Na Kanojo.


    This image is from Seishun Buta Yarou, it shows the scoreboard of a basketball game that happened during the anime.
    The name on the left side is 水明芸術大学附属高校, which means Suimei University Of The Arts. The school Kanda and Sorata attends.

Extra Notes:

I probably missed many Easter eggs but these are the currently well-known ones. I read up about these easter eggs through Reddit and also through watching over many scenes from the anime again. I do apologise if I missed any.
