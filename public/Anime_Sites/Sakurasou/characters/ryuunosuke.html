About Ryuunosuke Akasaka

Appearance : Ryuunosuke has an average build and height. He has extremely straight waist-length black hair and dark reddish-maroon eyes. He may even pass of as a girl due to his appearance being extremely feminine.
 
Japanese Name : 赤坂 龍之介
 
Gender : Male
 
Birthday : January 1st
 
Zodiac : Capricorn
 
Occupation : Programmer

About:
He is a main character in the anime. He is also a second year in Suiko. He is an introvert and rarely leaves his room. He communicates through emails/messages. He even programmed an AI called Maid to reduce the need for communication ,he provides lots of useful information to Sorata Kanda for his computer game. He also plans to make his AI into a real human. He has a condition called gynophobia which is a fear of women. This means he can pass out at the moment a girl touches him. He also loves tomatoes as he calls it a superfood
 
Personality :
Ryuunosuke is a more practical person than theoretical. He is extremely intelligent and can easily see through the real nature of people. Due to his introvertedness, he rarely cares about the feelings of others and hurt them several times due to his frankness. Though having a cold look most of the time, he is quite soft.
He is quite open with his feelings towards Rita. He fully utilises his skills but is also reserved, making him an extremely mysterious person. It is said that he became a recluse and wary of friendship because his previous friends shunned him when he gave honest but harsh criticism on their group project.

Friends

Sorata Kanda:
 
Seen on Sorata Kanda's Page
Ryuunosuke is shown to have a friendly relationship with Sorata, often asking for help in situations he is uncomfortable in. Though it is known that Sorata dislikes the part of Ryuunosuke that reveals the true nature of others. He calls Sorata "Kanda".
 
Maid:
 
Maid is Ryuunosuke Third Hand. She was made to help reduce Ryuunosuke's social interaction. Maid is very loyal to Ryuunosuke as he made her. Ryuunosuke plans to make Maid into a human. Potentially to help with his gynophobia?
 
Rita Ainsworth
 
Ryuunosuke shuns Rita away, due to his gynophobia and the thought that she was a freeloader. They started on bad terms but after awhile they started to grow closer. Rita starts to like Ryuunosuke, but Ryuunosuke, despite being an easy victim to her seduction, still runs away and hides from her. Though he is hiding, he will still listen to her if needed. As the anime processes, Ryuunosuke becomes more comfortable wwith Rita despite his gynophobia
