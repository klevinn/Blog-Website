About Sorata Kanada

Appearance : Sorata has an average build and height. He has short, spiked light brown hair and eyes. He is considered decent looking and he does not style his hair in any certain direction.
 
Japanese Name : 神田 空太
 
Gender : Male
 
Birthday : May 9th
 
Zodiac :Taurus

About:
He is the male protagonist of the anime. He is a second-year student of Suimei University of the Arts. He used to play soccer but stopped due to loss of interest. He was moved to Sakurasou after refusing to get rid of the white cat he had picked up. He felt plain and normal as compared to the other members of Sakurasou as they all are very talented. His whole life starts to change when he is suddenly put in charge of Shiina Mashiro. He is also known as Kouhai-kun by Misaki
 
Personality :
Sorata Kanda is very kind-hearted as can be seen by him not being able to leave abandoned cats alone, always taking them in. He is hot-headed and easily gets angry at people, especially Shiina. Sorata initially starts off as indecisive and unsure of his goals, but as he grows he starts to learn more about himself. He is a hard worker and learns not to give up, though being easily dejected by rejection.


Relationships

Family

Mother: Akiko Kanda
Father: Mr Kanda
Sister: Yuuko Kanda
 

Friends

Shiina Mashiro:
 
Sorata is Shiina Mashiro's caretaker, he uses most of his time to help her do things. Sorata occasionally compares himself to the talented Shiina Mashiro, and gets insecure of himself. Sorata does consider Shiina as pretty and is also very overprotective of her as her caretaker. He gets angry at her when she tries to do things other than drawing manga , Shiina's talent. He is also occasionaly frustrated at how awkward Shiina is.
Sorata does develop feelings for Shiina throughout the anime.
 
Nanami Aoyama:
 
Aoyama is Sorata's best friend in school, being in the same class for several years. Sorata admires the fact that Aoyama is very hardworking and dependable. Sorata is worried for her as she works too much to achieve her dreams but he also relates with her because they both are not as talented as others and work hard to achieve their dreams
 
Misaki Kamiigusa:
 
When first moving to Sakurasou, Misaki was the first to approach Sorata. Sorata was very tolerant of Misaki's upbeat personality and humours her most of the time, while playing games with her often. Sorata also cares deeply for her, trying to solve her problems when she needs help.
 
Yuuko Kanda:
 
As his sister, she has a lot of sisterly love for Kanda. However, this is very one-sides, Sorata finds her quite clingy and is annoyed by her "love". Despite this fact, Sorata cares for her deeply and tries his best to help her. For example, talking to their father to let her take the entrance exams to his school.
 
Chihiro Sengoku:
 
She is the teacher-in-charge of Sakurasou dorm. However , Sorata sees her as an extremely irresponsible and lax person. SO much so, he got used to her and does errands for her when asked. Sorata even ocassionaly makes fun of her for her outfits everytime she goes out
 
Rita Ainsworth:
 
Their relationship is not very significant but When Rita came to Japan, after being shut out by Mashiro, it was Sorata who came to her rescue and let her stay in his room. When Rita left for England, she was given Sorata's email address.
 
Jin Mitaka:
 
Sorata sees Jin as a massive playboy. However, he confides in him often. They are seen together at times talking about their problems, and helping each other when needed
 
Akasaka Ryuunosuke
 
Sorata and Ryuunosuke are similar in fields. Ryuunosuke is a programmer, while Sorata was a game designer learning programming language. Ryuunosuke speaks with Sorata through the internet sometimes, but not often because he is introverted. Sorata often gets angry at Ryuunosuke's frankness and is also shocked by the things he does, such as installing bugs on the dormmates phones.
