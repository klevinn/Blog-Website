About:
He is a main character in the anime. He is a third year in Suika. He is Misaki Kamiigusa's childhood friend and a giant playboy. He has dated several girls, including Misaki's Sister, but in the end he breaks up with all of them. He has feelings for Misaki but feel like he is out of her league, so he never accepts her until he feels he can match her
 
Personality :
Jin is laid-back and caring. He also has quite a shy personality. He has the ability to know a person's personality just by seeing how they work. Jin is very good with his words. His scripts are used for Misaki's anime, and the both of then usually collaborate to make an anime. However, his scripts have been considered by critics, and even Misaki herself, as subpar.

Misaki Kamiigusa
 
Jin cares about and loves Misaki a lot. Jin always has Misaki on his mind despite being a massive playboy. He constantly rejects her because he doesn't feel that he's good enough for her. He doesn't want to be swallowed up in Misaki's shadow, due to the gap that existed between them such as her art being highly praised while Jin's scripts are called average. It is also because he somewhat feels jealous about her successes, and wants to see Misaki get crushed.
Jin can also tolerate Misaki's shinnenigans despite being annoyed by it soemtimes
