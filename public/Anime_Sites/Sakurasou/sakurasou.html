Info About Anime

Also known as: さくら荘のペットな彼女 , The Pet Girl Of Sakurasou
Genre : Romantic Comedy , Drama
24 Episodes (Roughly 23min per episode)
Studio: J.C. Staff
Aired on: 09.10.2012 until 26.03.2013

Production Staff

Author: Kamoshida Hajime
Director: Ishizuka Atsuko
Character Design: Fuji Masahiro, Mizogushi Keiji
Animation Director: Ooki Ryouichi
Script: Kamoshida Hajime
Sound Director: Aketagawa Jin
Music: Hayashi Yuzo
Series Composition: Okada Mari

Synopsis

Sorata Kanada, the main protagonist and a student of Suimei High School, could not abandon the stray cats he found. Since the main dorms don't allow pets, he was forced to move into Suimei High School’s infamous Sakura Hall(Sakurasou), the satellite dorm.

Sorata finds it difficult to fit in with the bizarre collection of extremely talented dorm residents like Misaki, an energetic animator; Jin, a playwright playboy; Ryuunosuke, a reclusive programmer; and Chihiro, the dorm manager, art teacher, and party girl. His friend Nanami, a second year student and aspiring voice actress, pushes for him to go and find owners for these cats he picked up so that he can move back into the normal dorms.

However, The story starts when a girl named Shiina Mashiro, who is a very talented artist that wants to become a manga author, moves into the dorms. She is so obssessed with art that she can barely take care of herself, and Sorata then becomes the care taker of her. The Anime then shows them growing up as people and the many experiences they go through together with their friends and dormmates. Especially shows the development of Sorata

More about the story

The story may seem light-hearted at first starting of with many comedic scenes. However reaching the later parts of the anime, it is seen that it is tackling more real life issues. Such as a student struggling with finding their dream, life will not always be easy(showing through constant rejection) and more importantly it tackles the stereotypical mindset of how Talent always beats Hard Work

Personal Opinion

I feel that this anime is really worth the watch. Its one of my highest ranked anime among all that I have watched. I believe that the anime had a very good balance of Drama and Comedy, it also has a good touch of real world issues mixed with issues that normally will only be seen in shows/animations. Lastly to me it also is very relateable as it shows the conflict between Talent and Hard Work which is very evident in schools nowadays.
