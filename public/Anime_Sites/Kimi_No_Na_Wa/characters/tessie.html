About Teshigawara Katsuhiko

Appearance : Teshigawara is a young man who is quite tanned with an average build and height. He has thick dark eyebrows and hazel eyes. He has black hair thats normally shaved. As he grew older, he grew taller , still had a shaved head with a beard stubble thats growing.
 
Japanese Name : 勅使河原 克彦
 
Gender : Male
 
Birthday : January 18th 1996
 
Zodiac : Capricorn
 
Occupation : Unknown
 

Japanese VA Ryou Narita

Mandarin VA

About: Teshigawara Katsuhiko also known as Tessie by Mitsuha & Sayaka is a side character in Kimi No Na Wa. He is one of Mitsuha's close friends. He initially had a crush on Mitsuha . His parents forces him to take over the construction business one day which leads to him having a knack for machinery such as explosives.

 
Personality : Tessie is a happy-go-lucky person that gets flustered easily when speaking to Mitsuha. He is kind and willing to do anything for his friend. He also often complains how boring the place he lives in with nothing to do.
