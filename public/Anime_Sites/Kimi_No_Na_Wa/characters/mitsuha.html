About Mitsuha Miyamizu

Appearance : Mitsuha is a teenage girl with brown eyes of an average build and height who is described as very beautiful and the spitting image of her late mother, Futaba. She has straight, black hair which she wears two braids on each side tied into a small ponytail with a red, blue and yellow-orange-colored braided cord.
When she is older , Mitsuha grows out her hair to upper-back length, where she has her hair down with a small section of it tied into a braid at the back with the braided cord.
 
Japanese Name : 宮水 三葉
 
Gender : Female
 
Birthday : December 1st 1995 (Age Varies throughout the movie)
 
Zodiac : Saggitarius
 
Occupation : Shrine Maiden(Formerly)

About:
Mitsuha Miyamizu is the female protagonist of Kimi no Na wa. She lives in a small rural town, Itamori, where she serves as a priestess in her family shrine. She does not like her life in Itamori and wishes to live as a handsome boy in Tokyo. Her wish was granted as she would start switching bodies with Taki Tachibana.
 
Personality :
Mitsuha is a kind, caring, organized and supportive girl who is also determined and sometimes persistent, frank, as well as adventurous. She is tired of her lifestyle in the town of Itomori, she dislikes her family traditions and also has a greater interest in exploring outside her hometown. This is why when she starts switching bodies with Taki, she is very appreciative of everything while making the best out of it.
 
Mitsuha hated to perform her family traditions mainly because of her classmates always making fun of her. Mitsuha takes the belittlement from her father and her classmates withoutany form of retaliation. Mitsuha is also shown to be straightforward and persistent until what she wants has been done  
Romance-wise , Mitsuha was able to give decent advice to Taki. However there really is not much shown about it.
 
As an adult:
There is not much seen about her except her new appreance, and the fact she still wears the red headband.

Friends

Sayaki Natori :
 
Sayaki Natori is a side character. She is one of Mitsuha's closest friend, and she and Teshigawara risked themselves to help Mitsuha execute a plan she had thought up.
 
Katsuhiko Teshigawara :
 
Katsuhiko Teshigawara known as Tessie to Sayaki and Mitsuha is a side character that is also one of Mitsuha's closest friends. He is known to be good in machinery and used that experitse to help Mitsuha occasionally.  


Fun Facts!

She is seen in "Weathering In You"
She appears as a jewelry saleswoman for a shop in LUMINE and it is also said that the movies are linked.
 
Her name, Mitsuha (三葉) means "three leaves".
This also applies with her grandmother, her name meaning "one leaf"; her mother, "two leaves"; and her younger sister, "four leaves".
Her family name, Miyamizu (宮水) means "shrine water", kind of referencing the family's role as priestesses.
 
According to Yotsuha, when Mitsuha sleeps, it's very hard to rouse her. She also has frequent bouts of sleeplessness.
