About Sayaka Natori

Appearance : She has chestnut brown hair, which she wears in two braids pointing upwards and straight bangs. She has brown eyes and also has a mole directly under one of her eyes. As an adult, she shortens her hair and leaves it in a Bob Cut.
 
Japanese Name : 名取 早耶香
 
Gender : Female
 
Birthday : February 1st, 1996
 
Zodiac : Aqaurius
 
Occupation : Unknown
 

Japanese VA Aoi Yuuki
English VA Cassandra Lee
Mandarin VA

Hover to see his future self!

Currently No Voicelines


About: Natori Sayaka is a shy and quiet girl. She is close friends with Mitsuha and Teshigawara. She is a member of the school broadcasting club. She is known as Saya-chi by Mitsuha, which is just a nickname given to her.

 
Personality : She is calm, quiet and kind. Most of the time level-headed and thinks logically. She likes to disprove her attraction to Teshigawara. And like Teshigawara and Mitsuha, she also hates living in the small town of Itamori.
