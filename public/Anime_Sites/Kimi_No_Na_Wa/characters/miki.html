About Miki Okudera

Appearance : Okudera is an attractive lady with wavy mid-length maroon hair which she wears in a ponytail and amber eyes. She has a fair complexion and is often seen wearing red lipstick. Anywhere but her workplace , she normally lets her hair down or in another hair style. As she grew older, her facial features remain similar, but she normally leaves her hair untied.
 
Japanese Name : 奥寺 ミキ
 
Gender : Female
 
Birthday : January 22, 1992
 
Zodiac : Aquarius
 
Occupation : Waiter (Beginning)
 

Japanese VA Masami Nagasawa
English VA Laura Post
Mandarin VA Unknown

About: Miki Okudera is a side character in the movie Kimi No Na Wa. She was Taki's co-worker and Taki initially had a crush on her.Nothing much can be said about her. She was known as Okudera Senpai by Taki Tachibana, which basically means senior Okudera in Japanese

 
Personality : Okudera has a girly personality, purposefully being attractive. She likes smoking also stating that she is unable to stop
