About Taki Tachibana

Appearance : Taki Tachibana has an average build and average height who is considered good-looking. He has spiky brown hair and bangs that covers his forehead and a bit of his eyes, which are blue in colour. As he grows up, his facial features does not really change. However , he get taller and also broader shoulders.
 
Japanese Name : 立花瀧
 
Gender : Male
 
Birthday : December 1st 1998 (Age Varies throughout the movie)
 
Zodiac : Saggitarius
 
Occupation : Waiter (Beginning) , Architect (Ending)
 

Japanese VA Kamiki Ryuunosuke
English VA Michael Sinterniklaas
Mandarin VA Zhenji Huang

About:
Taki is the male protagonist in Kimi No Na Wa. He juggles his part-time job at an Italian Job with his school life, where he aspires to becomes an architect. His ordinary lifestyle suddenly had a massive change when he woke up and had swapped bodies with the female protagonist , Mitsuha Miyamuzu
 
Personality :
Taki is a kind, hardworking and caring individual. He is a very helpful and responsible person. He is organized and mostly handles everything quite nicely such as his busy lifestyle.
Since he's aspiring to be an architect he also has a creative side, also having good artistic abilities.
Taki is considered stubborn as he can be persistent. He may even be called sensitive and impatient as he gets angered quickly.
 
As previously mentioned , Taki is somewhat quick-tempered.Seen in the beginning of the movie, he gets into fights easily. He is also blunt and he tends to blurt out the first thing on his mind without restraint. However, he quickly fixes his way of speaking by saying what he really wants the second after. Since Taki hates belittlement from others, he is very straightforward and can stand up for himself and others.
 
Taki is also horrible at romance. Just like most of us!
 
Taki is a very determined person, as he explores the rural parts of Japan in order to meet Mitsuha in person, with only minimal knowledge of where she is or where to even begin finding her.
 
As an adult:
Taki has matured but still has his former personality. He has developed an even deeper fascination with architecture and Itomori, the place that disappeared.There is a little more to him as an adult but I would be spoiling a lot more, so watch the movie to find out!


Taki Tachibana Relationships

Family

Mother: Divorced (Stated by Producer)
Father: Mr Tachibana
Grandmother : Fumi Tachibana (seen in Weathering in You)
 

Friends

Mitsuha Miyamuzu:
 
Mitsuha Miyamuzu is the female protagonist. They met through the phenomenom of them suddenly switching bodies when they woke up. Their relationship started off rocky due to their disapproval of things they did in each other's bodies. However, throughout they became closer with a deeper relationship and understood each other better throught experiencing each other's lives. Mitsuha Miyamuzu then becomes Taki Tachibana's love interest at the later part of the movie
 
Shinta Takagi
 
Shinta Takagi is a side character, he does not have much screen time overall. However he is one of Taki Tachibana's closest friends. And they do stay friends all the way till the end of the movie.
 
Tsukasa Fuji
 
Tsukasa Fuki is a side character as well. However he has increasing amount of screen time towards the later part of the movie. He is also one of Taki Tachibana's closest friends, thats willing to do anything for him. For example, he was willing to help take Taki to the rural parts of Japan just to find Mitsuha that they have no proof of being real.
 

Co-Workers

Miki Okudera
 
Miku Okudera was Taki Tachibana's co-worker at an Italian Restaurant and was also Taki Tachibana's Initial Love Interest. They do start off as good friends and they even went on a date together. Miku Onodera and Tsukasa Fuji also were the 2 people that followed Taki when he was trying to find Mitsuha
 


Fun Facts!

In "Weathering In You"
Taki visited his grandmother's house to help her hold the Obon ceremony for his late grandfather. He met Hina Amano and Hodaka Morishima there and advised Hodaka to find a gift for Hina's upcoming birthday.
 
Taki has a tendency to clench his fist when embarrassed or nervous.
 
Taki's name (瀧), combines the characters for "water" and "dragon".
It shows a connection between himself and Mitsuha. Water (Mizu) is mentioned in Mitsuha's surname and since the Miyamizu folklore includes mention of a dragon.

Its a Romantic Drama Movie that i highly recommend. It is able to trigger many emotions in me and also is able to show the problems the characters are tackling in depth
