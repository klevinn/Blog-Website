About Tsukasa Fujii

Appearance : Tsukasa is a slim young man with an average build and height. He has dark blue eyes and black-blueish nape-length hair with bangs. His parting is more to the right instead of center. Hewears transparent rectangular-shaped glasses. However as he grows older, he gains sharper facial features and grew taller. He still wears rectangular specs however he gels his hair to the right now.
 
Japanese Name : 藤井 司

About: He is a side character in Kimi No Na Wa. He is one of Taki's closest friends and cares deeply about him despite making fun of / teasing him a lot.

 
Personality : He is caring but quite stern to his friends. He is also very supportive of his friends despite being worried for them. He is the perfect representation of an older brother figure especially to Taki
