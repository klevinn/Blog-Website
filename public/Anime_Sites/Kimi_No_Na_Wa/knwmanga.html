About Manga and Light Novels

A Short summary of what Manga and Light Novels are. Manga is basically just a Japanese comic, packed with drawings depicting the story of the Anime , normally their stories go further than the Anime. This is because Animes are basically the adaptations of Mangas most of the time. Light Novels on the other hand are story books that depict the story, having very few illustrations throughtout the whole series. As previously mentioned, Light Novels are adapted into Mangas and Animes.

Manga

The Manga , just like the Anime, is based of the Light Novel.
Info About the Manga

  • Volumes : 3
  • Chapters : 9
  • Authors : Makoto Shinkai (Story) , Ranmaru Kotone (Art)
  • Serialised by Comic Alive, Published by Kadokawa

Manga (Another Side:Earthbound)

About the Another Side:Earthbound

This manga is a version of the story that shows the daily life and activities of Taki when he switched bodies with Mitsuha. It showed more behind the scenes aspects that is probably not shown inside the Movie

  • Volumes : 2
  • Chapters : 14
  • Authors : Makoto Shinkai (Story) , Ranmaru Kotone (Art)
  • Serialised by Comic Alive, Published by Kadokawa
  • Volume 1 shows more of Taki being in Mitsuha's Body while Volume 2 shows more behind the scenes of the other characters that were involved


Release Dates:

  • Volume 1 :February 23, 2018
  • Volume 2: July 26 2019


Personal Opinion On Manga

I haven't read the manga! So I am unable to provide a personal opinion on this! However, I did read the manga for Another Side: Eartbound. It was a good experience as it explores more of the behind the scenes and show us what the characters experienced that was not scene throughtout the movie. It did try to apply aspects of suspense et cetera however it was a much lower scale as compared to the Movie. I do recommend it if you would like a better look at the personal life of the characters, but in my opinion it is more of a manga to pass the time.

Light Novel

About the Light Novel

The Light Novel is the primary source of the Kimi No Na Wa Story!
Info about the Light Novel

  • Volumes : 1
  • Chapters : 8
  • Authors : Makoto Shinkai & Arata Kanou (Story) , Masayoshi Tanaka & Hiyori Asakawa(Art/Illustrations)
  • Fully Released by Kadokawa
  • The movie has already fully portrayed the story in the novel so you will not be missing out on anything if you have already watched the movie


Release Dates:

  • Volume 1 - June 18, 2016


Novel (Another Side:Earthbound)

About the Another Side:Earthbound

  • Volumes : 1
  • Chapters : 4
  • Authors : Makoto Shinkai & Arata Kanou (Story) , Masayoshi Tanaka & Hiyori Asakawa(Art/Illustrations)
  • Published by Kadokawa
  • The Light Novel is basically a book version of the Manga! If you are a more visual person I recommend reading the manga instead of the Light Novel


Release Dates:

  • Volume 1 : August 1 2016


Personal Opinion On Light Novel

From my memory, I did read the Light Novel a long time ago. I do not recommend reading it. As previously mentioned, everything in the movie is already said in the Novel. Also, the experience of the movie was much better than the experience of the Light novel. This is because the movie have excellent visuals and animation. Unless you prefer reading over watching , I do not recommend reading / purchasing the novel for yourself. Overall , Watching the anime / Reading the manga would be much preferable.

Recommendation: A Silent Voice
Its a Romantic Drama Movie that i highly recommend. It is able to trigger many emotions in me and also is able to show the problems the characters are tackling in depth
